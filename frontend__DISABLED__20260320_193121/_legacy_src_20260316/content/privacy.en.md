# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-07

STELLCODEX processes account and usage data required to operate core features, including authentication, project/file handling, and service diagnostics.

We retain data only as needed for security, reliability, legal compliance, and user-requested functionality. We do not intentionally collect more personal data than necessary for these purposes.

Operational logs may include technical metadata (timestamps, request identifiers, status codes, and system events) to support debugging and abuse prevention.

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and security obligations.
