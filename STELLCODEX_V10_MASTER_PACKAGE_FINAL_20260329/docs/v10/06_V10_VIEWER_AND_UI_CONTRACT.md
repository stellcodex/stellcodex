## UI Freeze

The STELLCODEX V10 UI is frozen. No further redesigns, rebuilds, or refactors are permitted for the V10 lifecycle. This covers all frontend surfaces under `frontend/`.

## Locked Viewer Principle

STELLCODEX viewers are read-only review surfaces. They may inspect, annotate, and present artifacts but must never promise authoring or CAD editing capabilities.

## 3D Viewer Contract

- Orbit, pan, zoom.
- Part visibility and model tree controls.
- Inspection and highlight.
- Screenshot and presentation-safe viewing.

## 2D Viewer Contract

- Pan and zoom.
- Controlled server-side path for DXF viewing.
- No browser-side DXF source-of-truth requirement.

## Readiness and Truth

Viewer truth is dependent on deterministic artifacts:
- **Assembly Meta**: Required for assembly-aware workflows.
- **Deterministic Preview**: Must be generated by ORCHESTRA/STELL-AI logic.
- **Fail-Closed Viewer**: Viewer must not load if authorization fails or if the share is expired.

## UI Behavior Rules

- **Identity**: Show `file_id` only. Never leak internal paths or keys.
- **Progress**: Upload and status flows must expose progress and errors clearly.
- **Isolation**: Tenants must never see another tenant's files or projects.
- **RBAC**: Admin and operator routes must be protected by strict RBAC.
